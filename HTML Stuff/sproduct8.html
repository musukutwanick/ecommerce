Product Details

The Apple MagSafe Charger is a wireless charging solution designed specifically for iPhone models starting from the iPhone 12 and later. It features a magnetic alignment system that ensures a perfect connection between the charger and the device, allowing for efficient and reliable charging. With a power output of up to 15W, the MagSafe Charger provides faster wireless charging compared to standard Qi chargers, making it a convenient option for users who want to quickly power up their devices. The charger is also compatible with other Qi-certified devices, including AirPods and Apple Watch, although at lower charging speeds. The sleek and compact design of the MagSafe Charger makes it easy to use at home or on the go. It can be paired with a 30W power adapter for optimal performance, allowing users to take full advantage of its fast charging capabilities.
