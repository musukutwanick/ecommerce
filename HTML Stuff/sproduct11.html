Product Details

The Apple TV functions primarily as a digital media player and streaming device, allowing users to access a wide range of streaming services, including Apple TV+, Netflix, and more. It is equipped with advanced hardware, such as the A15 Bionic chip in the latest models, which enhances its performance and decoding capabilities for high-quality video and audio playback. While the Apple TV is not a traditional decoder like those used for receiving broadcast signals (e.g., DVB T2 HEVC decoders), it can decode various audio formats, including Dolby Digital Plus and Dolby Atmos. This means that when streaming content that supports these formats, the Apple TV can decode the audio and send it to compatible audio receivers or sound systems as a multi-channel PCM stream. Additionally, there are discussions about future Apple TV models potentially incorporating dedicated AV1 decoding capabilities, which would enhance video streaming efficiency and quality. However, as of now, the Apple TV primarily serves as a streaming box rather than a conventional decoder for broadcast television signals.
